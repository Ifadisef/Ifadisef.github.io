About Us

ERMIS PMS PC was founded in 2024, bringing with it 20 years of experience in the maritime industry, both on land and at sea. The company possesses specialized knowledge in various sectors, from tankers and cargo ships to performing demanding tasks on cruise ships and yachts.

Aiming to provide high-quality services, ERMIS PMS PC focuses on innovation and technological advancement, constantly seeking the most effective ways to improve its services. With a dedication to safety and environmental sensitivity, the company continually seeks ways to enhance its practices that contribute to environmental sustainability and the protection of marine ecosystems.

Pilotage Services in Katakolo Port

We specialize in providing comprehensive pilotage services at the port of Katakolo, emphasizing a steadfast commitment to safety and efficiency.

Led by our experienced Master Mariner Class A, our team ensures that every vessel receives meticulous support and guidance during its entry to or departure from the port. This involves:

  • Safety Focus: Prioritizing the safety of vessels, crew, and cargo above all else. Our procedures are designed to minimize risks and ensure smooth operations.
  • Efficiency: Streamlining maritime procedures to prevent delays and optimize turnaround times for vessels. We aim to maintain a reliable and punctual service for all our clients.
  • Expertise: Leveraging extensive maritime knowledge and experience to navigate vessels through the port safely, even under challenging conditions.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Ensuring adherence to all relevant maritime regulations and protocols, guaranteeing that every operation meets international standards.

Our commitment is to provide a dependable and efficient service environment at the port of Katakolo, ensuring smooth and safe navigation for vessels while upholding the highest standards of professionalism and reliability.

Inspections

We provide comprehensive support and ship inspections that align with the latest industry regulations, including Class, Vetting, Pre-vetting, ISM-ISPS, pre-purchase assessments, and more. Our suite of services includes:

  • Detailed Inspections: Conducting thorough examinations of vessels to identify any potential issues and ensure compliance with all relevant standards.
  • Compliance Reporting: Preparing detailed reports that outline the vessel's adherence to regulations and highlight any areas needing attention.
  • Safety Assurance: Ensuring that all aspects of the ship's operation meet the highest safety standards to protect the crew, cargo, and environment.
  • Regulatory Adherence: Keeping up-to-date with the latest international standards and regulations to ensure that every vessel meets current compliance requirements.
  • Customized Solutions: Providing tailored support to address the specific needs of each vessel and operation, from initial assessments to ongoing maintenance and inspections.

Our goal is to deliver peace of mind by ensuring that your vessels are safe, compliant, and operating at peak efficiency, in accordance with the most recent international standards and regulations.

Voyage Operational Planning and Expense Forecasting

We provide meticulous voyage operational planning, including expenses forecasting, to ensure efficient and cost-effective operations. This includes:

  • Route Optimization: Choosing the most efficient route to minimize travel time and fuel consumption.
  • Cargo Handling: Ensuring safe and compliant loading, stowage, and unloading procedures.
  • Crew Management: Organizing schedules and responsibilities to maintain smooth operations and compliance with rest period regulations.
  • Regulatory Adherence: Guaranteeing that all voyages meet international and local maritime regulations.
  • Emergency Preparedness: Developing comprehensive contingency plans for potential emergencies.

By integrating these services, we help you achieve optimal operational efficiency and financial performance, ensuring your vessels are always in line with the latest standards and best practices.
